Lung Cancer Prediction

Info : Lung cancer is a type of cancer that begins in the lungs and is the leading cause of cancer deaths worldwide. People who smoke have the greatest risk of lung cancer, though it can also occur in people who have never smoked. Symptoms typically include a new cough that doesn't go away, coughing up blood, shortness of breath, chest pain, and unexplained weight loss. Early detection significantly improves prognosis.
