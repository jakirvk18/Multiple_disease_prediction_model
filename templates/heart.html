Heart Disease Prediction

Info : Heart disease refers to a range of conditions that affect your heart. Diseases under the heart disease umbrella include blood vessel diseases, such as coronary artery disease; heart rhythm problems (arrhythmias); and heart defects you're born with (congenital heart defects), among others. Symptoms often include chest pain, shortness of breath, and fatigue. Lifestyle changes, medications, and sometimes surgery can manage heart disease. Early detection and intervention are crucial for preventing serious complications.
